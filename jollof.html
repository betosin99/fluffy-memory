JOLLOF RICE

Jollof rice! A beloved West African dish that's simple, flavorful, and steeped in tradition. Here's a breakdown of the ingredients and steps to prepare this iconic one-pot wonder:

Steps To Prepare

  1. Rinse the rice: Rinse the rice in a fine-mesh sieve until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside
  2. Heat oil in a pot: Heat the oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat
  3. Saut√© onions: Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they're translucent and starting to caramelize (about 5 minutes)
  4. Add garlic and ginger: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir well and cook for 1 minute
  5. Add tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree) and stir well. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes start to break down
  6. Add spices:Add the cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine
  7. Add water: Add the water to the pot, bringing the mixture to a boil
  8. Add rice: Add the rinsed rice to the pot, stirring to coat the rice with the tomato mixture
  9. Reduce heat and simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.
  10. Fluff with a fork: Once the rice is cooked, remove the pot from the heat. Fluff the jollof rice with a fork to separate the grains
  11. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh herbs (like parsley or cilantro) and accompanied by your favorite protein (like chicken, beef, or fish)
