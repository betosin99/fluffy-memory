YAM PORRIDGE

Yam porridge, a beloved Nigerian dish! Here's a breakdown of the ingredients and steps to prepare this comforting, flavorful dish:

Ingredients;

To prep Yam Porridge, you need the following;

  • 2-3 large yams, peeled and chopped into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons fish or beef stock (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato puree (optional)
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped (optional)

Steps To Prepare

  1. Boil the yams: Place the chopped yams in a large pot and add enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the yams are tender.
  2. Sauté the onions: Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they're translucent and starting to caramelize.
  3. Add garlic and ginger: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir well and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add spices and seasonings: Add the cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add water and stock (if using): Add the water and stock (if using) to the pot with the boiled yams. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Mash the yams: Use a potato masher or a fork to mash the yams in the pot.
  7. Add tomato puree (if using): If using, add the tomato puree and stir well to combine.
  8. Simmer and thicken: Simmer the porridge for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until it has thickened to your liking.
  9. Serve: Serve the yam porridge hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro (if desired).
