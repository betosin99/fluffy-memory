EBA & EGUSI SOUP

Like a match made in heaven, the duo combos are for the ages

Ingredients;

To prep Eba, you need the following;

  • 2 cups cassava flour (or garri)
  • 1 cup hot water (right after boil)

Steps To Prepare

  1. Mix flour and water: Gradually add the hot water to the cassava flour in a large bowl.
  2. Knead the mixture: Use your hands to knead the mixture until it forms a smooth, pliable dough.
  3. Rest the dough: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 5-10 minutes
  4. Knead again: Knead the dough again until it's smooth and elastic.
  5. Shape into small balls: Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of a small orange

Dough is now ready to be served but first, lets get the egusi prepared first

Ingredients;

To prep Egusi soup, you need the following;

  • 2 cups ground melon seeds (egusi)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped (or 2 cups tomato puree)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons fish or beef stock (optional)
  • Leafy greens (such as spinach or bitter leaves), chopped (optional)

Steps to prepare

  1. Roast the egusi: Dry-roast the ground melon seeds in a pan over medium heat, stirring frequently, until fragrant and lightly browned.
  2. Heat oil in a pot: Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Sauté onions: Add the chopped onions and sauté until they're translucent and starting to caramelize.
  4. Add garlic and ginger: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Stir well and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree) and stir well. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add spices and roasted egusi: Add the cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Stir well. Add the roasted egusi and stir to combine.
  7. Add water and stock: Add the water and stock (if using). Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes
    8. or until the soup has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  8. Add leafy greens (optional): If using, add the chopped leafy greens and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. Serve: Serve the Egusi soup with eba.

The voracious meal is now ready to be enjoyed over any choice of beverage you wish to have with it
